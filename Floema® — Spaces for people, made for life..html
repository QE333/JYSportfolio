About Floema

Rooted in nature.
Designed for people.

Floema is the living fabric of a company born in the heart of Portugal. We design signage, furniture, and equipment — not as isolated objects, but as extensions of the landscapes they inhabit.

Every product carries the intention of permanence. Materials are chosen for how they age, how they weather, how they become part of a place over time. This is design that lives alongside you — through every season, every use, every year.

Sustainability

Sustainability comes last — intentionally.

Floema is already defined by its materials and reverence for nature. Important, yes — but contained, not used as a loud marketing weapon.
